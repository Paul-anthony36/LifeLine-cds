LifeLine School

It was the vision of Pauloos Bahadur that there must be a school for the children of those families thatwe were visiting. Therfore, in January 2003, there was a small meeting of 10 people. After a discussion of a few hours it was finalised that we wree going to start a new school. The name of the school was to be LifeLine Christian school. On 1st February,2003 school was started with almost fifty children of Jogi Goth. We only had 3 teachers, 3 rooms and one helper at that time. In the beginning, our school only operated as a primary school, but after 4 years we registered as a middle school as well. We spent 10 years in a small school of 7 rooms in Nusrat colony. Then, by the grace of God, a new plot was gifted to us in 2009 and new school building was built on January of 2003.The new school building was inaugarated by Br.Hans Martin and Br. Matthias. Since then, lifeLine school has partnered with Agha Khan university board, and we are also working on offering 9th and 10th grade levels as well. Aim of the school is to provide free education to those students who are underprivileged and have no access to proper education. Most of our students are on full financial aid and others pay as little fees as possible. Our plan for the year 2020 is to start 9th grade under the supervision of Sindh Board. We are grateful to God as our school annually participates in National events, Christmas programs and other local events as well. LifeLine school provides the facilities of science lab, computer lab and access to books free of cost to all students.
